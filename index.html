Newton’s
Cannon

Newton's Cannon is a thought experiment used to motivate orbital motion. For low speeds, the cannonball doesn't get far relative to the Earth's size, and the downward gravity gives a parabolic path. At higher speeds, the cannonball goes far enough that the direction of gravity always toward the center of the Earth changes, and so does the shape of the path. wikipedia link
